❓ Common Questions
Why isn't the bot responding in my group?
Make sure: (1) your number is connected on this dashboard, (2) the bot number is added to the group, (3) you're using the correct prefix (.)
Why can't the bot kick/mute users?
The bot needs to be an admin in the group to do group management actions. Go to Group Info → Participants → long press the bot → Make Admin.
How do I set up welcome messages?
Use .welcome on to enable welcome messages. Customize with .setwelcome [your message]. Use @user for the member name and @group for the group name.
How do I use the AI feature?
Just type .ai [your question] in any chat. Example: .ai What is the capital of Nigeria? The AI remembers context for up to 3 messages.
